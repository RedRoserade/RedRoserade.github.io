Things I've made

Portal of the Photographic Archive of Silva Magalhães

A website dedicated to showcasing the contents of the Photographic Archive of Silva Magalhães. This project came to fruition through a partnership through the Polytechnic Institute of Tomar and the Council of Tomar.

"Letrinhas"

An Android application for young students to help them improve their reading skills in a fun way. I lead the webservice and back-office teams, which were tasked of handling the database and the API and the input of data, respectively.

The application is currently being tested in schools throughout the district of Santarém.
